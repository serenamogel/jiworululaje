Sajal Ali Career

Sajal initially appeared on film in a tiny part in an episode of Geo TV's comedy drama Nadaaniyaan in 2009. She was praised for her breakthrough performance in the 2011 ARY Digital family drama Mehmoodabad Ki Malkain. She gained to notoriety after starring in various popular television shows, including the comedy Mohabbat Jaye Bhar Mein, the romances Sitamgar and Meri Ladli, the family comedy Quddusi Sahab Ki Bewah, as well as the drama Gul-e-Rana.

Her work in the teen drama Nanhi, the psychological thriller Sannata, the retribution drama Chup Raho, and the spiritual romance Khuda Dekh Raha Hai gained her great acclaim and nominations for the Lux Style Award for Best Actress. Sajal received critical recognition for her roles as a disturbed youngster in the telefilm Behadd and as an aspiring actress in her debut feature film, Zindagi Kitni Haseen Hay.
