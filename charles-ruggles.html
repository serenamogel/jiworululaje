Biography

Charles Ruggles was born in Los Angeles, CA on February 8, 1886. Longtime Hollywood actor who played the hunter, Major Applegate, in the screwball comedy, Bringing up Baby. In 1935, he appeared with Bing Crosby in Anything Goes. He was one of the narrator voices behind the Rocky and Bullwinkle TV cartoon – he provided the voice of the Aesop’s Fables segment, while William Conrad narrated. His Hollywood debut came in the 1914 film, The Patchwork Girl of Oz.
