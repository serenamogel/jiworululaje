FIFA 23 TOTY: Voting News, Release Date Updates &amp; Leaks

Here is everything you need to know about FIFA 23's Team of the Year (TOTY) in Ultimate Team.

First Published December 16, 2022, 17:38

As the 2022 world cup draws to a close, tons of promotional content is set to hit FIFA 23. From the Path to Glory, to the Road to the World Cup items, there is no shortage of things to collect this holiday season. Now that the year is almost over and the world cup winners are determined, players are starting to look toward the Team of the Year. This is the annual and most anticipated promotional squad of the season, and this year will be no exception. Created to celebrate those players who left the biggest impact, the 2022 Team of the Year is likely to be one of the best squads yet!

So, if you are looking for more information about FIFA 23's Team of the Year in Ultimate Team, you've come to the right place. This article will cover everything you need to know about the TOTY promo!

FIFA 23 Team Of The Year (TOTY) Ultimate Team Release Date

At the time of writing this, the FIFA 23 Team of the year release date has not been confirmed. That being said, based on the previous game's Team of the Year launch, we can speculate about what the most likely date for this promo to go live will be. Check out some of the previous launch dates for Team of the Year below:

  • FIFA 22: January 21, 2021
  • FIFA 21: January 22, 2020
  • FIFA 20: January 6, 2019

As you can see, the Team of the Year is almost guaranteed to launch in January. According to rumors at Insider Gaming, the Team of the Year is set to run from Tuesday, January 10, 2022, until Wednesday, February 1, 2023. While nothing is confirmed, expect to see the release sometime in January. 

What Is Team Of The Year (TOTY) In FIFA 23?

Essentially, the Team of the Year is exactly what it says in the name. This is a squad comprised of the best eleven players of the year. EA Sports decides on this list, but there will also be the twelfth man as voted in by the community of FIFA players.

Familiar faces will surely return, such as Messi, Ronaldo, and MBappé are almost certain to make the cut, but we are looking forward to seeing if any newcomers manage to enter the FIFA 23 Team of the Year this time. 

And that's everything you need to know about the TOTY in FIFA 23!
