Cale Gundy Salary: How much does Cale Gundy make a year?

Joseph Cale Gundy played quarterback for the Oklahoma Sooners from 1990 to 1993. From 1999 until 2022, he was an assistant coach at his alma mater, first as running backs coach and later as the team’s offensive coordinator. Gundy resigned his position on the coaching staff on August 7, 2022.

How much does Cale Gundy make a year?

According to USA Today, assistant coaches are paid an average of $200,000 per year. Therefore, it is fair to say Cale Gundy is also paid a similar value.

Joseph Cale Gundy has already coached a lot of teams. Some of them include;

    • Oklahoma (GA)
  • 1995 UAB (QB)
  • 1996–1998 UAB (RB)
  • 1999–2004 Oklahoma (RB)
  • 2005–2014 Oklahoma (RB/RC)
  • 2015–2016 Oklahoma (AHC/IWR/RC)
  • 2017–2021 Oklahoma (OC/IWR/RC)
  • 2022 Oklahoma (WR)

Kristy Gundy is best known as the adorable, supportive, and beautiful wife of Cale Gundy. Kristy Gundy is believed to be a lawyer from Norman, Oklahoma, with a great reputation.
