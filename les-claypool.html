Biography

Les Claypool was born in Richmond, CA on September 29, 1963. Lead vocalist and bassist of the band Primus who has created many side projects, including Colonel Les Claypool’s Fearless Flying Frog Brigade. His album Les Claypool and the Holy Mackerel presents: Highball with the Devil features Henry Rollins. He auditioned for Metallica as Cliff Burton’s successor but did not make the band. He worked as a carpenter before forming Primus.
