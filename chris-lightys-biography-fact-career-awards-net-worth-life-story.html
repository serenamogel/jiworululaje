American executive Chris Lighty has an estimated net worth of $30 million. He was known as the co-founder of the marketing and management company, and record label Violator, which has represented artists like Mobb Deep, Ja Rule, Nas, L.L. Cool J, Missy Elliott, 50 Cent and Uncle Murda, In the past, Lighty has served as the manager of Sean “Diddy” Combs. He was once considered by the New York Times as “one of the most powerful figures in the hip-hop business.”

Darryl Steven Lighty was born on May 8, 1968, in The Bronx, New York City, New York, U.S. At the age of 44, he died on August 30, 2012. He was found lying face-up on his apartment in The Bronx with a 9mm pistol beside him. Some of the people closest to him questioned the possibility of suicide.
