Team Rocket's Handiwork
ロケット団の工作 Rocket Gang's Handiwork

Team Rocket's Handiwork (Japanese: ロケット団の工作 Rocket Gang's Handiwork) is a Supporter card. It is part of the Fates Collide expansion.

Card text

Flip 2 coins. For each heads, discard 2 cards from the top of your opponent's deck.
You may play only 1 Supporter card during your turn (before your attack).

Release information

This card was included as both a Regular and a Full Art card in the Fates Collide expansion, first released in the Japanese Awakening Psychic King expansion. Both prints feature artwork by Hitoshi Ariga.

Gallery

Trivia

Origin

This card depicts both male and female Team Rocket Grunts, a Trainer class, as featured in Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver.
