The Meaning Behind The Song: Dream On by Aerosmith

One of the greatest rock bands of all time is Aerosmith. This American band has been active since the 1970s and has produced a number of smash hits over the years. One of their most popular songs is called “Dream On,” which was released in 1973 and has since become a classic rock anthem. But what was the song really about? In this article, we will explore the meaning behind “Dream On” by Aerosmith.

  • The Lyrics and Their Meaning

Background and Inspiration for the Song

“Dream On” was written by the lead singer of Aerosmith, Steven Tyler, when he was just 17 years old. At that time, Tyler was struggling to find his way in the world and had been kicked out of his home by his parents. He started living in an apartment with his bandmate, Joe Perry, and they began working on their music together.

The inspiration for “Dream On” came from Tyler’s own experiences and feelings of uncertainty and vulnerability. The lyrics of the song are deeply personal and reflect his desire to make something of himself and achieve his dreams despite the obstacles in his path.

The Lyrics and Their Meaning

The opening lines of “Dream On” set the tone for the rest of the song: “Every time that I look in the mirror / All these lines on my face getting clearer / The past is gone / It went by like dusk to dawn.”

These lyrics suggest that the singer is reflecting on his life and how quickly time seems to be passing him by. He is aware that he is getting older and that his dreams may be slipping away, but he is determined not to give up.

The chorus of the song is perhaps the most famous part, with Tyler’s high-pitched vocals reaching their peak: “Sing with me, sing for the years / Sing for the laughter and sing for the tears / Sing with me, if it’s just for today / Maybe tomorrow, the good Lord will take you away.”

These lyrics are a reminder to live in the moment and to enjoy life while we can. They also suggest that music can be a source of comfort and inspiration, even in times of hardship.

In the second verse, Tyler sings: “Dream on, dream on, dream on / Dream until your dreams come true.” This line is the crux of the song and encapsulates its message. Tyler is urging the listener to never give up on their dreams, no matter how impossible they may seem. He is suggesting that anything is possible if we believe in ourselves and keep working towards our goals.

The Impact of “Dream On”

When “Dream On” was first released in 1973, it wasn’t an immediate hit. But over time, it became a classic rock song that has endured for decades. It has been covered by many artists and used in numerous movies and TV shows, cementing its place in pop culture.

The song’s message of perseverance and hope has resonated with audiences around the world. It has become an anthem for anyone who has ever struggled to achieve their dreams and overcome obstacles in their path. “Dream On” is a reminder that even when things seem impossible, we should never stop dreaming.

In Conclusion

“Dream On” by Aerosmith is a timeless classic that has stood the test of time. Its message of hope and perseverance has inspired countless people over the years and continues to do so to this day. Steven Tyler’s personal journey and struggles are reflected in the lyrics of the song, making it all the more powerful and relatable.

As Tyler sings in the chorus of the song: “Sing with me, if it’s just for today / Maybe tomorrow, the good Lord will take you away.” Let us all take inspiration from these words and make the most of every moment. Let us never give up on our dreams and always keep pushing forward.
