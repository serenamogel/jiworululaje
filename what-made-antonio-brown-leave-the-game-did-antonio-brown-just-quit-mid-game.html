Antonio Brown is an American football wide receiver for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers of the National Football League. He was born on July 10, 1988, in Miami, Florida.

Brown attended Miami Norland High School. He played college football at Central Michigan University, where he earned All-American honours in 2008 and 2009 as a pint returner. In 2019, he was traded to the Oakland Raiders who made him the highest-paid receiver in the league.
