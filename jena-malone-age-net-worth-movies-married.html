Who is Jena Malone?

Jena Malone is an American actress, musician, and photographer. She grew up in a low-income family. They frequently changed their living place from one place to another. They also had to live in cars and hotels from time to time. She had already lived in 27 different places by the time she was nine years old.

She takes all the experiences from her childhood very positively. All the problems that she faced as a child prepared her for the events that she is facing now. It prepared her for the strange lifestyle of an actor. From her childhood experiences, she learned that security is within one’s own self, not inside four concrete walls.

After watching her mother perform in the community theater, she became interested in acting. In 1995, she traveled to Las Vegas and started taking acting classes. She also convinced her mother to move to Los Angeles.

She often mentions that they had faced financial problems during their stay in Los Angeles. From sixth to eighth grade, she was homeschooled, and after that, she attended the Professional Children’s School in New York for ninth grade.

How old is Jena Malone?

Jean Malone was born on November 21, 1984, in Sparks, Nevada, US. Her father’s name is Edward Berge, and her mother’s name is Deborah “Debbie” Malone. She was a single mother. During her childhood, she was raised by her mother and her mother’s girlfriend, whom she called ‘Godmom.’  Her grandfather was Karl Berge, the Karls Silver Club’s previous owner.

Jena Malone Movies

She has played in several popular movies. Some of them are:

  • Donnie Darko (2001)- It is a 2001 science fiction film directed by Richard Kelly. Jena Malone plays the character of Gretchen Ross in the movie. The movie has received wide critical acclaim.
  • Pride and Prejudice (2005)- It is a 2005 romance film directed by Joe Wright. Keira Knightley is the lead actress in the movie. Jena Malone plays the role of ‘Lydia Bennet’ in the movie. It was successful at the box office and has garnered positive reviews.
  • Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice (2016)- You might wonder when you see Jena Malone in the movie. That’s because she wasn’t in the theatrical cut and was only there in the Ultimate Edition home media release. In it, she had a minor role as Jenet Klyburn, a scientist at the DC universe STAR Labs.

Besides these movies, she has also appeared in Into the Wild (2007), Sucker Punch (2011), and The Hunger Games Series (2012-2015).
