Are Jayson Tatum And Draymond Green Related? Relationship

As these two professional basketball players are in the news for the last few days, people wonder- are Jayson Tatum and Draymond Green related?

Jayson Tatum is an American professional NBA player who attended Chaminade College Preparatory School in St. Louis.

He was widely regarded as one of the country’s top high school basketball players.

After graduating, Tatum played college basketball at Duke University for one season before declaring for the NBA Draft.

In the 2017 NBA Draft, Boston Celtics took Tatum as the third overall pick. He still plays for Celtics.

Meanwhile, Draymond Green is also an American NBA player who began college basketball at Michigan State University and was a Spartan standout player. 

In the 2012 NBA Draft, Green was selected by the Golden State Warriors as the 35th overall pick.

Relationship Explained: Are Jayson Tatum And Draymond Green Related?

No, Jayson Tatum and Draymond Green are not related. They are both professional basketball players in the NBA but do not share a familial relationship.

Tatum currently plays for the Boston Celtics, while Draymond is a member of the Golden State Warriors.

Draymond Green and Jayson Tatum have developed a close friendship over the years, evident by their interactions both on and off the court.

Despite being on rival teams, Green and Tatum have maintained a strong bond, which was showcased when Green invited Tatum to his wedding the previous year.

Their friendship endured despite the intense competition between the Boston Celtics and the Golden State Warriors during the 2022 NBA Finals.

This was demonstrated by Green’s playful interaction with Tatum’s son, Deuce, while Tatum was being interviewed after a game.

Their friendship has grown over time and seems to be unaffected by external factors, such as social media controversies involving other players.

More On Jayson Tatum And Draymond Green: Married Life

Jayson Tatum was born to young parents, Brandy Cole-Barnes and Justin Tatum, who were both 19 years old at the time.

His mother primarily raised him, although he also has a half-brother and half-sister on his father’s side.

Despite being a single mother, Brandy Cole-Barnes, Tatum’s mother, achieved a law degree in St. Louis, Missouri.

His father, Justin Tatum, used to play basketball and currently coaches a high school team in St. Louis.

Tatum’s son, Jayson Christopher Tatum Jr., known as Deuce, was born on December 6, 2017, to Tatum and his high school girlfriend, Toriah Mimms.

Likewise, Draymond Green also takes great pride in his role as a son, husband, and father, often sharing glimpses of his family life on social media. 

He is married to Hazel Renee Joiner, with whom his relationship dates back to their time at Michigan State University, where they initially met in a theater class.

The duo welcomed their first daughter named Cash in 2020, and after two years, they exchanged their wedding vows on August 14, 2022.

A heartwarming video shared by Renee on October 26, 2020, captured their return home from the hospital with baby Cash.

Apart from Cash, Green has a son named Draymond Jamal Green Jr. “DJ,” born on December 22, 2016, with his ex-girlfriend, Jelissa Hardy.

Likewise, Hazel Renee also has a daughter named Olive from a previous relationship with Jacob Pullen. It appears that Olive primarily resides with her mother.
