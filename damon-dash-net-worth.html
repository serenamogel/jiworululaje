What is Damon Dash’s net worth?

Damon Dash, the American record producer, entrepreneur, and music executive, has a net worth of $100 thousand, a far cry from his one-time partner Jay-Z’s billionaire status. Dash co-founded Roc-A-Fella Records with Jay-Z and co-founded two clothing lines, including Rocawear, which was sold for hundreds of millions of dollars. Dash also ventured into film production, marketing, merchandise, and talent management.

Despite his successes, Dash has faced several financial issues in recent years. In 2019, he told a New York judge that he was too broke to pay child support, and most of his income was being garnished by various creditors. Dash has also faced legal issues, including being sued for releasing music without permission and being accused of sexual assault, which was mostly cleared by a federal jury in early 2023.

Dash has had several high-profile relationships, including with R&B singer Aaliyah, whom he planned to marry before her death in 2001. He was also married to Rachel Roy, with whom he had two daughters, leading to a bitter custody battle and accusations of domestic abuse.
