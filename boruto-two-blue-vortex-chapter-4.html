The recent release of Boruto Two Blue Vortex reveals new enemies and problems, as chapter 4 sets a different mood for the rest of the story. With spoilers circulating online, fans discuss spoilers for the manga.

Moreover, enemies like Code and Grimes launch assaults on the village from the beginning.

As chapter 4 promises an intense battle, readers look for the spoilers of Boruto Two Blue Vortex.

Kashin Koji made an exciting appearance in the spoilers of chapter 4 of Boruto Two Blue Vortex.

Koji is a clone of Jiraiya that Amado Sanzu created to kill Isshiki Otsutsuki.

Moreover, he was in charge of the sector in the outskirts of the Land of Fire as a former Inner of Kara.

Meanwhile, as a clone of one of the Sannins, Koji is regarded as a complete monster in terms of power and ability.

Additionally, people viewed him as a troublesome opponent of the Sixth Hokage.

Likewise, Koji is a master of Space-Time ninjutsu, which Boruto displayed in a recent chapter.

Along with that, Koji’s teaching Boruto draws a parallel between the plot of Jiraiya and Naruto.

Similarly, the clone has his reasoning for guiding Boruto.

Many readers have assumed that the true goal of Koji lies in his intentions to defeat Code.
