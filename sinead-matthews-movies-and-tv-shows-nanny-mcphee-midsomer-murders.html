Sinead Matthews Movies and TV Shows, Nanny McPhee, Midsomer Murders

Award-winning actress Sinead Matthews appeared in many films such as Nanny McPhee Returns, The Boat That Rocked, Happy-Go Lucky, Mr. Turner, Wreckers, Hogfather, and Jellyfish.

On television, Matthews starred in The Crown (Marcia Williams), Black Mirror (“Be Right Back”), Midsomer Murders, Pride & Prejudice, Hullraisers (Paula), and many more.

Sinead Matthews Nanny McPhee

Matthews plays Miss Topsey, a strict and uptight tutor, in the sequel, “Nanny McPhee Returns.”

While not the magical nanny herself, Miss Topsey plays a role in helping the unruly children learn lessons through her own experiences and eventual softening demeanor.

Sinead Matthews Midsomer Murders

Matthews appeared in a single episode of Midsomer Murders titled “The Wolf Hunter of Little Worthy” in 2021.

She played the character of Mel Wallace, a woman involved in a local environmental campaign.

About Sinead Matthews

Sinead Matthews, the accomplished English actress, has graced screens and stages for over two decades with her versatile performances.

Born in Coventry in 1980, Matthews refined her acting skills at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art, laying the foundation for a remarkable career.

Known for her diverse roles, Matthews has seamlessly navigated through period dramas like “Pride & Prejudice” (2005), the regal world of “The Crown” (2019), and the whimsical realm of Terry Pratchett’s “Hogfather” (2006).

Her repertoire extends to dark comedy in “Black Mirror” (2013) and beloved series like “Ideal” (2005-2010) and “Hullraisers” (2022).

Beyond the screen, Matthews is a seasoned theater artist, captivating audiences with performances in Ibsen’s “The Wild Duck” and Annie Baker’s “Till the Stars Come Down.”
