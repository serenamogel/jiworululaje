How To Complete Lair Sweet Lair In Disney Dreamlight Valley

Want to appease the infamous sea witch Ursula in Disney Dreamlight Valley? Learn how to complete the quest Lair Sweet Lair in this guide.

First Published October 23, 2022, 15:33

Disney Dreamlight Valley is a sim-like game where you can interact with all your favorite Disney and Pixar characters, ranging from its many heroes to villains. One of the most well-known villains is the sea witch Ursula from The Little Mermaid. 

The Lair Sweet Lair quest in Disney Dreamlight Valley tasks you with welcoming the infamous sea witch; while the quest is relatively easy to complete, gathering all the necessary items might take some time. This guide will cover how to complete the Friendship quest, Lair Sweet Lair, in Disney Dreamlight Valley.  

Disney Dreamlight Valley - How to complete Lair Sweet Lair

The Disney Dreamlight Valley Lair Sweet Lair is a quest you will receive from The Little Mermaid villain, Ursula. After unlocking Ursula, you must provide her with a proper lair. To do this, you must craft a Magical Scroll and Ink Kit to complete this quest. 

Here are the following materials you will need to craft the kit: 

  • 6x Dream Shards

  • 2x Purple Pigment

  • 1x Empty Vial

To make Purple Pigment, you will need 8x Purple Failing Penstemon and 8x Purple Rising Penstemon. The Penstemons can be found in the Plaza of Disney Dreamlight Valley. After you have gathered these flowers, you can craft them into Purple Pigment at your Crafting Station.

To make an Empty Vial, you will need 3x Glass. Unfortunately, you won’t be able to find Glass in Disney Dreamlight Valley, but you can craft it with 5x Sand and 1x Coal; you can find these items on Dazzle Beach.  

You are then required to gather 3x Paper. You can find the first Paper in Goofy’s house; more specifically, it will be on the sofa. Next, you can find the second Paper inside Mickey Mouse’s house on the table. Finally, you can find the third Paper in Merlin’s house on the table. All of their houses are located in the Peaceful Meadow. 

Note: If you cannot enter Mickey’s home, it means he is asleep. In this case, you will have to wait for him to wake up. 

Once you have crafted the Magical Scroll and Ink Kit, go back to Ursula’s Castle and give the items to her. After completing the Lair Sweet Lair quest in Disney Dreamlight Valley, she will reward you with two rare fish: 1x Bream and 1x Shrimp, as well as 180x Friendship Points.

That concludes our guide on how to complete Ursula’s Friendship quest.
