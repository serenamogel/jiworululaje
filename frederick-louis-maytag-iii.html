When did Fritz Maytag Buy Anchor Brewing?

On Aug. 2, 1965, a young Stanford grad named Fritz Maytag purchased a controlling interest in Anchor Brewing, a nearly defunct San Francisco brewery that had been founded in 1896. Anchor produced steam beer — a style created during the Gold Rush using a lager yeast that ferments at higher temperatures.
